Richard Banasiak


Software Engineer and Mobile Technology Evangelist

Richard Banasiak is a software engineer with over 15 years of experience in mobile, enterprise and network security software development. He is passionate about emerging mobile technology and its ability to positively impact the lives of end users. He has led development of cutting edge platforms including augmented reality (AR), voice control, wearables, beacons and embedded Internet-of-Things (IoT) connectivity.

Richard is a proven leader who engages directly with clients starting with concept ideation through the entire design and development process. He has worked closely with the product stakeholders of several high-profile apps in the insurance, retail, healthcare, real-estate and IoT industries to ensure solutions were delivered on-time and within budget constraints.

Richard constantly aims to solve complex problems using creative solutions that would make his childhood idol, MacGyver, proud.
